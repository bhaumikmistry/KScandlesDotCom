Our Story

At KS Candles, we believe that every space deserves to be filled with warmth, comfort, and beautiful fragrance. Founded with a passion for creating the perfect ambiance, we hand-pour each candle using only the finest ingredients.

Our candles are crafted from 100% natural soy wax, sourced sustainably and blended with premium essential oils. Each candle burns cleanly for up to 50 hours, filling your home with long-lasting, authentic scents that transport you to your favorite memories.
